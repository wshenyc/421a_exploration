FAQ
What's this tool for?

This tool helps tenants understand potential rent surcharges and changes to rent stabilization in buildings with expiring 421-a tax exemptions. It brings together information from the Department of Finance, Department of Buildings, and HPD, which can otherwise be difficult to access.

Where are you getting your data/info from?

Property tax exemption data comes from a DOF open dataset. Construction/permit data comes from a DOB open dataset and DCP's Housing Database.

421-a specific info comes from HPD's fact sheet, HCR's FAQ, and DOF's 421-a phaseout table.

Can I see your code?

Absolutely! For the code I used to make this page and my data analysis/cleaning process, check out the Github.

I have questions about my specific apartment. Can you help?

Probably not unfortunately, but I'd recommend calling Met Council on Housing's tenants' rights hotline.
