# Map of 421-a Buildings in NYC

This [tool](https://wshenyc.github.io/421a_exploration/) is aimed to help tenants understand potential rent surcharges and changes to rent stabilization in buildings with 421-a tax exemptions. It brings together information from the Department of Finance, Department of Buildings, and HPD.
